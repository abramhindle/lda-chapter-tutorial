LDA Tutorial

Topic analysis has become a hot topics in software engineering
research, especially mining software repositories research. It is
popular because often researchers have to sift through numerous
documents and try to make sense of 1000s of bug reports, commits, or
source files.

In this LDA Tutorial participants will get hands-on experience with
LDA and get some valuable LDA tools. We will go through the steps of
collecting and extracting documents, cleaning them up for processing
by an LDA tool, producing topics and then evaluating topics.


Audience members should preinstall virtualbox and vagrant.
